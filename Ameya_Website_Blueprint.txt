1. Theme & Visual Direction
Based on your logo and branding, the recommended theme is Luxury Dark — a premium, editorial aesthetic that mirrors the gold-on-black identity of the Ameya logo. Here is the full rationale and style specification.
1.1  Recommended Theme: Luxury Dark Gold
This is the strongest match for your brand identity. The gold metallic logo on black background immediately signals premium positioning. Your competitors in digital marketing typically use bland blue/white themes — the Luxury Dark theme will make Ameya stand out memorably.
Theme Specification
Theme Name
Luxury Dark Gold
Primary Background
#0A0A0A (Near Black) — creates depth and contrast
Secondary Background
#1A1A1A (Dark Charcoal) — for cards and panels
Primary Accent
#C9A84C (Gold) — all CTAs, highlights, icons, borders
Light Accent
#E8C97A (Light Gold) — hover states and gradients
Text Primary
#FFFFFF (White) — headings and bold text
Text Secondary
rgba(255,255,255,0.55) — body copy and descriptions
Display Font
Cormorant Garamond (serif) — headings, logo, hero
Body Font
Jost or DM Sans (light weight) — paragraphs, nav, UI
Aesthetic Tone
Luxury / Editorial — minimal, spacious, premium
Inspiration
High-end consultancy, luxury fashion, premium tech agencies
1.2  Why This Theme Works for Ameya
Your logo is gold on black — the website must be an extension of that identity
Dark backgrounds increase perceived premium value in B2B services
Gold accents create strong visual hierarchy — everything important pops
Cormorant Garamond gives sophisticated editorial presence
Spaced-out layouts with generous whitespace signal confidence
Stands out completely from typical blue/purple digital marketing sites

The white horizontal logo (Image 2) should be used for: email signatures, letterheads, printed materials, and any light-background digital collateral. The website itself uses the dark version exclusively.

5. Technical Recommendations
5.1  Platform Options
Recommended Tech Stacks (Choose One)
Option A — WordPress
Best for: content updates, blog, SEO. Use Elementor Pro or custom theme. Recommended for non-technical teams.
Option B — Webflow
Best for: premium design control, animations, no-code. More expensive but stunning results. Recommended if budget allows.
Option C — Next.js (Custom)
Best for: full control, performance, custom features. Needs a developer. Future-proof and fastest.
Our Recommendation
Webflow for a premium feel with manageable maintenance, OR WordPress + Elementor for cost-effectiveness.
5.2  Essential Integrations
Google Analytics 4 — track all traffic, pages, conversions
Google Search Console — monitor search rankings and indexing
WhatsApp Chat Widget — instant contact for Indian market
Meta Pixel — track Facebook/Instagram ad conversions
Calendly — optional, for direct booking of strategy calls
Mailchimp or Brevo — newsletter and email marketing
Hotjar — heatmaps to see how visitors use the site
5.3  SEO Foundations
Each page has a unique meta title and meta description
URL structure: /about, /services, /work, /blog, /contact
Blog articles target keywords like: 'digital marketing agency Dehradun', 'social media management India'
Google My Business listing linked to the website
Schema markup for local business and services
Site speed optimised: images compressed, lazy loading enabled
Mobile responsive design — tested on Android and iOS
